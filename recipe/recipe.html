Blueberry Muffins

Based on this recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil, olive oil, or melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Grease the muffin tin or line the tin with muffin papers.
  3. Blend together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  4. Blend together the milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the latter wet ingredients into the former dry ingredients.
  6. Briefly whisk the mixture with a fork or wire whisk. Do not overstir clumps that may appear.
  7. Throw the berries into the mix.
  8. Fill the cups of the muffin pan 2/3 of the way each because the dough will rise. (Optional: top each muffin with crystallized or powdered sugar)
  9. Place muffins on middle baking rack.
  10. Bake muffins for 15-20 minutes. Insert a toothpick into the center of a muffin to test if the dough is still sticky. The muffin is ready when the toothpick comes out clean.
  11. Do not eat them immediately. They will scald your mouth.
